the serve is the first shot of a point in a tennis game. each player serves for an entire game. players begin by standing at the baseline, tossing the ball overhead, and aiming for the diagonal box called the service box. a serve that lands in the service box is in. a serve that lands beyond the service box or in the net is called a fault. players are given two serves per point.

the return is the second shot of a point in a tennis game. whoever is not serving for the game will be the reciever. receivers will stand at the baseline diagonal to the server. they can return using a backhand or a forehand, depending on where the serve lands. returns are usually a defensive shot. a game won by the returner is called a 'break' and puts them at an advantage.
